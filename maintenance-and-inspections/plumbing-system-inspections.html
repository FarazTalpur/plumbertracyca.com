3. Water Heater Assessment

A malfunctioning water heater can disrupt your daily routine. Our inspection services include evaluating all types of water heaters, from tankless to conventional models, to ensure optimal performance and extend their lifespan.

4. Pipe and Fixture Examination

Over time, pipes and fixtures can corrode or break. Regular inspections help in spotting weaknesses early on. Our team checks for signs of wear, recommending replacements or repairs to prevent future problems.

Frequently Asked Questions

It is recommended to have your plumbing system inspected at least once a year to ensure everything is functioning properly and to catch any potential issues early.

During a plumbing inspection, a professional plumber will check for leaks, inspect pipes, assess water pressure, and examine fixtures and appliances to ensure they are in good condition.

To prepare for a plumbing inspection, clear any clutter around plumbing fixtures, make a list of any known issues, and ensure that all areas are accessible to the inspector.

Common issues include leaks, low water pressure, blocked drains, corroded pipes, and faulty water heaters. Addressing these early can prevent more serious problems.

The cost of a plumbing inspection varies based on the size of the home and region, but typically ranges from $100 to $500. It's best to get quotes from several plumbers for accurate pricing.
